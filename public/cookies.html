1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to site owners.

Cookies cannot damage your device or access other files stored on it.

Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies such as:

  • Local Storage: Browser data storage
  • Session Storage: Temporary session data
  • Pixel Tags: Small images for analytics

2. How We Use Cookies

On the Swarve Music website, we use cookies to:

  • Remember your browsing preferences
  • Keep you logged in if you have an account
  • Analyze how our website is used
  • Improve user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
