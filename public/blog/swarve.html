Celebrating 500 Servers with Swarve Music

We've reached an incredible milestone thanks to our community. Discover what we have planned to celebrate and the new features coming soon.

Today, the wait is over! We are thrilled to announce the **official launch** of **Swarve Music**, the premium music experience for your Discord server.

After months of dedicated development, testing, and refinement, our goal of creating the best music bot for Discord communities is now a reality. We invite you to add Swarve Music to your server today and experience the difference: high-quality audio, multi-platform support, and seamless control.

Our Vision and Technology

Swarve Music was built from the ground up to address the biggest pain points of music bots: latency and reliability. We are launching with a robust infrastructure designed for **99.9% uptime** and ultra-low lag, ensuring your music never skips a beat.

Launch Day Official Opening
<50ms Latency
HD Quality Audio Standard
99.9% Guaranteed Uptime

These features are our foundation. We believe that a superior music bot experience starts with uncompromising speed and stability.

Commitment to the Community

The core of Swarve Music will always be its community. While we haven't reached 500 servers yet, we are committed to building this platform *with* you. Our early users and founders will play a crucial role in shaping the features and direction of the bot moving forward.

We encourage everyone to join our official support server. Your feedback, suggestions, and feature requests are essential, and we are ready to listen from Day 1.

Launch Day Highlights

To celebrate our official debut, we are launching with a full suite of features ready for you to use:

Founders Event Giveaway

We're kicking off our launch with a giveaway for early adopters! Join our support server for details.

Exclusive Founder Badge

The first users to add the bot will receive the special "Swarve Founder" badge on their profile!

Advanced Audio Filters

Launch with a wide variety of audio filters (like Bassboost and Vaporwave) available from day one.

A Message from the Team

Thank you for being here at the start of our journey. We are incredibly excited to deliver the high-quality music experience your Discord server deserves. Add Swarve Music today and let the music begin!
