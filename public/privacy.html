1 Introduction

At Swarve, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot ("Swarve Bot") and related services.

By using Swarve Bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our services.

Our Commitment

We are committed to processing data lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner. We follow privacy-by-design principles and collect only what is necessary for our services.

2 Data We Collect

We collect different types of data depending on how you interact with Swarve Bot. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of the data we may collect:

Automatically Collected Data

Data Type Description Purpose
User ID Your unique Discord identifier Account identification, feature delivery
Server ID Unique identifier of servers using Swarve Server-specific configurations
Username Your Discord username and discriminator Display purposes, logging
Command Usage Commands executed and timestamps Analytics, abuse prevention

Feature-Specific Data

Feature Data Collected Status
Leveling System XP points, level, message count, voice time Optional
Moderation Warnings, bans, kicks, mutes (with reasons) If Used
Custom Commands Command triggers and responses Optional
Reminders Reminder content and scheduled times Optional
Server Settings Prefix, enabled features, channel configurations Required

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Message content (except for command processing, not stored)
  • Direct message conversations
  • Voice chat audio or recordings
  • Personal information beyond Discord-provided data
  • Financial information or payment details
  • Location data or IP addresses

3 How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery - To provide and maintain Swarve Bot's features and functionality
  • Personalization - To remember your settings, preferences, and server configurations
  • Feature Operation - To operate specific features like leveling, moderation logs, and custom commands
  • Analytics - To understand usage patterns and improve our services
  • Abuse Prevention - To detect and prevent spam, abuse, and violations of our Terms
  • Communication - To respond to support requests and send important service updates
  • Legal Compliance - To comply with applicable laws and regulations
No Advertising

We do not use your data for advertising purposes or to create advertising profiles. Your data is used solely to provide and improve our services.

4 Data Storage & Location

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Database - Data is stored in encrypted databases hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
  • Location - Our servers are primarily located in the United States and European Union
  • Encryption - All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3
  • Backups - Regular encrypted backups are maintained for disaster recovery
  • Access Control - Strict access controls limit who can access stored data
Security Standards

We follow industry best practices including regular security audits, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing to ensure your data remains protected.

5 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers - With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our services (e.g., hosting providers), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements - When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Safety - To protect the safety of our users, staff, or the public
  • Business Transfers - In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice)
  • Consent - With your explicit consent for any other purpose

Third-Party Services

Swarve Bot may integrate with third-party services. When you use these integrations, their respective privacy policies apply:

  • Discord Privacy Policy
  • Music streaming services (when using music features)

6 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data from our systems.

Restriction

Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Objection

Object to certain types of data processing activities.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through:

  • Our Discord support server
  • Email: support@nexabro.site
  • Using the /privacy command in any server with Swarve

We will respond to your request within 30 days. Some requests may require identity verification.

7 Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

Data Type Retention Period
Server configurations Until bot is removed from server + 30 days
Leveling data Until deletion requested or server removal
Moderation logs 1 year (configurable by server admins)
Command logs 90 days
Error logs 30 days

When Swarve Bot is removed from a server, all server-specific data is automatically scheduled for deletion within 30 days.

8 Children's Privacy

Swarve Bot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by Discord in their jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Parental Notice

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@swarve.bot and we will promptly delete such information.

9 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption - AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Controls - Role-based access with principle of least privilege
  • Authentication - Multi-factor authentication for all administrative access
  • Monitoring - 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection systems
  • Auditing - Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident Response - Documented incident response procedures

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to promptly notifying affected users in case of a data breach.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • For significant changes, we will notify users through our support server
  • We may also send announcements through the bot for major updates
  • Continued use of Swarve Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

11 Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Discord Support Server: Join our server
  • General Support: support@nexabro.site
  • Website: swarve.xyz
Response Time

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 72 hours. For data access or deletion requests, please allow up to 30 days for processing.
